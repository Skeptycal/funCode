The Birds and the Bees

  1. Find a quiet seat in the garden.
  2. Press the timer button.
  3. Each time you observe a new hummingbird enter the garden click the button for the bird. Do the same for each bee you observe.
  4. When the timer goes off, record the final numbers in your garden journal.
